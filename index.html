Privacy Policy for Kinlog

Effective Date: April 2026

Kinlog respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Kinlog app.

Information We Collect

Kinlog may allow users to store information they enter into the app, such as names, relationship labels, notes, interaction logs, dates, reminders, and optional profile details.

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to provide the app’s core features, such as organizing people, tracking interactions, showing relationship maps, and managing reminders and logs.

Data Storage

Data is stored locally on the user’s device unless otherwise stated in future versions of the app.

Data Sharing

Kinlog does not sell or share personal information with third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

Kinlog does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

User Control

Users can edit or delete their stored data at any time within the app.

Children’s Privacy

Kinlog is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
