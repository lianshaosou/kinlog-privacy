Kinlog ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information Kinlog processes, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Process

Depending on how you use Kinlog, the app may process:

  • Contact profile data you create (for example: names, notes, categories)
  • Entry logs you create and link to contacts
  • Optional location data attached to logs (if you choose to add location context)
  • Follow-up reminders and scheduling data
  • Shared contact data that you explicitly choose to export or send
  • App settings (such as language, preferences, appearance options)

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to provide and improve app functionality, including:

  • Displaying and organizing your contacts and logs
  • Linking logs to people and showing timeline/history views
  • Enabling follow-up reminders
  • Enabling optional map/location context
  • Supporting backup, restore, and sync features (if enabled by you)
  • Supporting user-initiated sharing of selected contacts

We do not use your personal data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is primarily stored on your device. If you enable cloud backup/sync features (such as iCloud), your data may also be stored in services provided by Apple under your Apple account and Apple's terms.

4. Data Sharing

Kinlog does not sell your personal information. Data is shared only:

  • When you choose to share/export selected contacts or logs
  • When Apple platform services process data needed for app operation (for example, iCloud or notifications)
  • When required by applicable law

5. Permissions

Kinlog may request permissions such as:

  • Location (optional): To attach place context to logs
  • Face ID / device authentication (optional): To protect app access if you enable app lock
  • Notifications (optional): For follow-up reminders

You can change permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains available until you delete it, delete the app, or disable related cloud storage. If you use cloud backup/sync, retention may depend on your Apple account settings and Apple policies.

7. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your data. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Kinlog is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Because most data is stored locally, you can typically manage or remove data directly in the app or on your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

11. Contact Us
